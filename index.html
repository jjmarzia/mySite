Joseph Marziale


I am a first year PhD student in aerospace engineering with a concentration in continuum physics and applied mathematics at the University at Buffalo under the advisement of James Chen and David Salac. Currently I am a member of the Multiscale Computational Physics Lab working on the LIFT project. Previously I studied physics (BS '22) and mathematics (BA '22) at Roberts Wesleyan College under the supervision of Candice Fazar and Gary Raduns.
