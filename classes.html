Classes

I am currently developing typeset versions of notes I take for my classes. They are a work in progress, so much of what is contained in the table of contents is incomplete. Still, I thought that they might be helpful to myself and maybe to others. So hopefully I can finish them during break.

  • Continuum mechanics. Mathematical preliminaries, kinematics of deformable bodies, conservation and balance laws, thermodynamics of continua, constitutive relations, heat conduction in rigid solids, and mechanical theory of solids and fluids.
  • Engineering analysis. Linear algebra, ODEs, Fourier analysis, and PDEs.
  • Finite element analysis. 1-D Euler-Bernoulli beam theory; 2-D constant strain triangle (CST), 2-D isoparametric (QUAD4) and 3-D isoparametric (HEX8) elements.
